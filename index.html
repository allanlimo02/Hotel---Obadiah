Welcome To

The Concord Square Hotel

The Concord Square Hotel & Club is located in the renowned Maasai Mara Game Reserve, home to the great wildebeest migration. The property features an outdoor swimming pool, lounge and restaurant and exciting game drives accompanied by the most scenic sundowner cocktails and dining experiences in the bush.


The Outside Of Our Hotel

Here guests will find a lodge where everything is as it ought to be: two intimate camps of just fifteen tented suites each, a private airfield and access to the Mara below, tailor-made safari days and a famously warm Kenyan welcome.

Our Conference Hall

Conference room facility available for social and business functions of people between 20 to 25 people. Guests get to have their meeting surrounded in outstanding art.

Our Guest Rooms

Guests are accommodated in spacious guest Suites, which have been designed for guests’ comfort and are reminiscent of a classical African safari, each with a view as lovely as the next. Every detail has been considered: end-to-end glass frontage, sweeping canvas, decks overhanging the Mara, and inside – polished parquet floors and just the right amount of furniture.
